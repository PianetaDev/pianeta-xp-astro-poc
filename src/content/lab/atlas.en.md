## What Atlas is

Atlas is the proprietary ESG framework from Pianeta.Studio. It holds together the impact metrics we use across our projects — emissions, supply chain, social, governance — with a shared vocabulary between the studio, our clients, and our suppliers.

## Where it lives

The current version is online here: **[atlas.pianeta.green/atlas](https://atlas.pianeta.green/atlas)**.

From there you can navigate the full framework, the sections by dimension (E · S · G), the indicators, and the operational guidelines we follow inside the studio.
