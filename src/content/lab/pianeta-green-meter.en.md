## What GreenMeter is

GreenMeter is the tool Pianeta uses to measure the **CO₂ footprint** of websites — our own and our clients'.

We use it as a **carbon budget** instrument inside the design and development pipeline: every project has a threshold in grams of CO₂ per visit, and it meets it the same way it meets an accessibility or performance spec.

## What it measures

- **Page weight** transferred (HTML, CSS, JS, images, fonts) — gzip-aware
- **CO₂e per visit** following the Sustainable Web Design Model
- **Grade from A+ to F** benchmarked against the Web Almanac
- **Per-page breakdown** of the measured site

## Where it lives

[**pianeta.green**](https://pianeta.green) — free, no signup, in-browser measurement.

## How it connects to Atlas

GreenMeter is one of the indicators feeding into the [Atlas](https://atlas.pianeta.green/atlas) framework on the E (Environment) dimension — "digital footprint" column.
