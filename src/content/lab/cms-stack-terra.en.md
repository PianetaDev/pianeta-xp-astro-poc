## What Stack Terra is

Stack Terra is the **proprietary CMS** Pianeta uses to build our clients' sites. Not a white-label: a technical foundation designed around the way we work — co-design, AI validation, carbon budget as a verifiable constraint.

## Composition

- **Payload CMS** as the backoffice — schema-as-code, role-based access, REST/GraphQL APIs out of the box
- **Nuxt 3** for delivery — server-side rendering, hybrid static/SSR, edge-friendly
- **MeiliSearch** as the search engine — typo-tolerant, latency-aware
- **Carbon-neutral ISO 27001 hosting** with public impact metrics
- **Integrated CO₂ calculator** (GreenMeter) with a verifiable grade

## What we deliver to the client

- **Open standards**: no proprietary lock-in, exportable schemas and content
- **Code delivered**: the repo belongs to the client, not to us
- **Verifiable performance budget and carbon budget**, written into the contract
- **Italian backoffice** with flows tailored to the client's team
