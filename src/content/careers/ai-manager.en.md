## What you'll do

The studio already runs **20 AI agents as digital employees**: CFO, sales, design-system guardian, brand, sustainability. Each one with its own morning shift. They'll grow to 30+ by year-end. We need someone who can orchestrate them as a real team — not as a garden of prompts.

### The three things that will really matter

**1. Designing flows.** Mapping which decisions go through AI, which through people, which loop back to whom. Documenting every handoff.

**2. Measuring quality.** A quarterly review for each agent: what it produced, where it got things wrong, what needs updating in its system prompt. Tracking out in the open.

**3. Growing the human team alongside the agents.** AI doesn't replace people here. It frees them from repetitive work so they can do what matters.

## Who we're looking for

Someone with one foot in **day-to-day AI practice** (Claude, GPT, agentic workflows, RAG) and one foot in **operations** (processes, decisions, people). No PhD required. We need someone who has actually run a real team or a real agent pipeline.

## What we offer

Contract to be defined together — employee or partner. Based in Milan or Ardea (Pianeta.Farm lab) or remote. Enterprise tools. A place where **AI Safety** isn't a slide: it's written into the company charter.
