## Who it's for

For organizations competing in saturated markets, or that have shifted their value proposition and need to communicate it clearly.

## How we work

Competitive audit (1 week) + positioning workshop (2 days) + message writing (1 week).

## What the client gets

A defensible position with clear messages, ready to use on the website, in sales, and in investor decks.
