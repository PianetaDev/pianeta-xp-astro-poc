## Who it's for

For research centers, institutions, and multi-brand organizations that need a coherent visual system applicable to scientific publications, institutional communications, social, and events.

## How we work

Co-design with the communication manager. Three iteration cycles tested on real materials (a report, a LinkedIn post, a sample slide). 8-12 weeks.

## What the client gets

A brand manual written so a new team member can apply it correctly within two days of onboarding. No implicit decisions.
