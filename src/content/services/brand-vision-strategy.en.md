## Who it's for

For organizations entering a phase of transformation — new positioning, merger, international expansion, transition to a B Corp / Benefit Company model — that need to put their long-term trajectory into words before translating it into communication and product.

## How we work

Three one-week cycles, with incremental workshops and shared working materials. The final deliverable is an operational document, not a mood board.

## What the client gets

A document the team can actually use to make decisions — not a PDF to shelve. The narrative is wired to channels (who speaks to whom, where, in what tone).
