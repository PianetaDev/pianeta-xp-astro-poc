## Who it's for

For organizations that suspect their identity is misaligned, fragmented across departments, or no longer fit for their growth goals.

## How we work

Asset audit + stakeholder interviews + quantitative consistency analysis (eyeball + tooling). 2-3 weeks.

## What the client gets

An honest diagnosis with clear priorities. Often the first step toward a rebrand or a more disciplined design system.
