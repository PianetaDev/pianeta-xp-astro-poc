## Who it's for

For research centers, NGOs, and educational institutions that need to make a complex concept accessible to very different audiences without compromising scientific rigor.

## How we work

Workshops with researchers + rapid prototypes + validation with real audiences (teachers, classrooms, test readers). 12-20 weeks.

## What the client gets

A visual system that outlives a single book — it becomes the foundation for related projects without starting from scratch.
