## Who it's for

For creative teams that want to lower the risk of bad calls on high-stakes concepts — video campaigns, product launches, communications on sensitive topics.

## How we work

Ad-hoc panel setup (50-200 agents) + simulation + analysis. 2-3 weeks per cycle.

## What the client gets

A quantitative second opinion that engages with the team's intuition. No "AI deciding for you."
