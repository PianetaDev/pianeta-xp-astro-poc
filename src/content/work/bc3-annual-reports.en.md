## The challenge

The **Basque Centre for Climate Change** produces a significant volume of scientific research on climate change every year — peer-reviewed papers, Horizon Europe participation, policy projects. The annual report is the moment when this mass of research has to **speak to very different stakeholders**: funders, policymakers, academic partners, and the general public.

The challenge: turn a technical report that risks sitting on a shelf into a **communication tool that sparks conversations**.

## The objective

Design the **2022 and 2023** annual reports so they:

- Communicate research findings clearly
- Sustain stakeholder engagement
- Reflect BC3's commitment to climate research and sustainability (including physically, in print)
- Work as an ecosystem across **paper + digital + social**, not as separate assets

## The approach: data storytelling across channels

**1. Visual consistency across years.** The 2022 report laid the foundations — clean layout, infographic system, recognizable palette. The 2023 report built on that base, refining the print design and expanding data visualizations to **highlight the variations** between the two years of research.

**2. Print + digital + social as an ecosystem.** For each year:

- **Printed report** in a curated editorial format (eco-friendly: recycled paper, low-impact inks, printers with certified chain of custody)
- **Dedicated digital landing page** summarizing the main sections, with highlights, infographics, and key takeaways
- **Social campaigns** with visual highlights from the report for stakeholders and the broader public

**3. Storytelling through visualizations.** For 2023 we introduced **advanced data visualization techniques** inside the printed piece to emphasize the variations in research findings. The report becomes **readable as a story**, not a statistical appendix.

## What we delivered

### 2022 Report
- **Print** — clean modern layout, custom infographics that simplify dense scientific data
- **Dedicated digital landing page** that expands the document's accessibility

### 2023 Report
- **Print** — a refined redesign building on the 2022 edition, with expanded data visualization
- **Digital landing page** with improved navigation and dynamic infographics
- **Dedicated social campaigns** for each report

## The outcome

- **Reinforced brand identity** — consistency across print, digital, and social consolidated BC3's positioning as a reference research center
- **Reusable system** — the editorial pattern became the standard for future annual report editions, with no need to start from scratch
- **Message accessibility** — the 2023 report generated more social interaction and more landing-page traffic than 2022
- **Sustainability in production** — paper, ink, and printing choices aligned with BC3's mission
