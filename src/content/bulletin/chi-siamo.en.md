## About us

Pianeta.Studio is a creative and strategic studio founded in September 2023, building on the trajectory Max Mauro has been pursuing since 2016. It is a **Benefit Corporation** headquartered in Sicily, with operating offices in Milan and Ardea (Rome).

We work end-to-end — from research to delivery — across **food, agri, sustainability, and impact communication**, alongside an in-house product line we call Pianeta.\*.

## What we believe

**Planet-centric design.** This isn't a marketing line: it's written into our charter. Every project decision must hold up to the same question — *does it generate benefit for people and the biosphere, or not?*

**Co-design.** The best outcomes happen when the client is a co-author of the project, not a spectator.

**AI validation, UX validation.** Validate before producing. Save months of wrong turns with AI research technologies, before choices are locked in.

**Open standards. Code delivered.** No lock-in. What we build for you stays yours.

**Carbon budget and performance budget as verifiable technical constraints.** Measured, not declared.

## What we do

Three poles working together, one single discipline: **Design & Technology**.

- **Pianeta.Studio** — the digital world. Consulting, design, engineering.
- **Pianeta.Farm** — agriculture and production, in Ardea.
- **Pianeta.Lab** — research and experimentation, the connector between the two.

And three proprietary product lines we fund through client work:

- **Pianeta.Green** — proprietary CMS for digital sustainability, carbon-neutral hosting, GreenMeter to measure CO₂ per page.
- **DocumentAI** — workflow and tools for research documents with AI.
- **LabNeuromarketing** — AI validation of concepts with MiroFish Swarm and fMRI neural prediction.
